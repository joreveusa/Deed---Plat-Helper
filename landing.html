Core Features

Every tool a surveyor needs — unified

Stop switching between tabs, PDFs, and GIS software. Deed & Plat Helper brings your entire research workflow into one fast, focused workspace.

🔍
Deed & Plat Search
Search grantor/grantee indices with smart relevance scoring. TRS context, adjoiners, and client matches are surfaced automatically — not buried in 300 rows of noise.
🔗
Chain of Title Tracer
Follow ownership back through time automatically. Each link in the chain is clickable, bringing up the parent deed so you never lose your place in a complex title search.
📄
AI OCR & Field Extraction
Drop a deed PDF and instantly extract calls, bearings, distances, legal descriptions, and grantor/grantee — ready to paste into your survey software or a DXF boundary.
🗺️
Live Parcel Mapping
Search any owner name and instantly see their parcel highlighted on a county GIS map. Perfect for PLSS section checks, neighbor identification, and boundary visualization.
📐
DXF Boundary Export
Parse metes-and-bounds calls from OCR output and export a closed DXF traverse. Import directly into CAD — no manual coordinate entry, no transcription errors.
🧑‍🤝‍🧑
Adjoiners & Neighbors
Identify adjoining landowners automatically from the parcel layer and run deed searches for each. Knock out an entire adjoiner page in minutes instead of hours.
Simple Workflow

Set up in minutes. Research in seconds.

Connect once to your county portal, then search, map, and export without ever leaving the app.

1
Connect Your County
Enter your county's deed index URL. The onboarding wizard auto-detects the field mapping — takes about 90 seconds.
2
Search & Research
Search by name, book/page, or parcel. Results are ranked by relevance. Click any deed to open it directly in the county portal.
3
Extract & Export
OCR the deed, extract calls, and export a DXF boundary — or save plats directly to your document cabinet.
