Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Red Tail Surveying / Deed & Plat Helper

Short version: We collect only what's needed to run the service. We don't sell your data. You can delete your account at any time.

1. Who We Are

Deed & Plat Helper ("the Service," "we," "us," or "our") is operated by Red Tail Surveying. The Service is a professional research tool for licensed land surveyors and title researchers, hosted at deed-plat-helper.onrender.com.

Questions? Email us at joreveusa@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account data: When you register, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We never store your plain-text password.

Usage data: We track the number of searches you perform per month to enforce plan limits. We store your recent search history (last 20 queries) so you can review and re-run past work.

Payment data: Subscription payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number — only a Stripe customer ID and subscription status.

County portal credentials: If you configure a county records portal connection, your portal username and password are stored encrypted in our database. These credentials are used solely to perform deed searches on your behalf.

Uploaded files: PDFs you upload for OCR processing are processed in memory and not permanently stored on our servers.

Log data: Our hosting provider (Render) automatically logs HTTP request data (IP address, browser type, pages visited). These logs are retained for up to 30 days.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To authenticate your identity and enforce subscription plan limits
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To perform county deed searches using your configured portal credentials
  • To send transactional emails (password resets, welcome emails, subscription receipts)
  • To notify you of important changes to the Service or your account

We do not use your data for advertising and do not sell or rent your information to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

User account data is stored in a JSON-based database on Render's infrastructure (hosted in the United States). Access to this data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

We implement industry-standard security practices including:

  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt (one-way, salted)
  • HTTPS enforced on all connections
  • Auth tokens with expiration (30-day rolling sessions)
  • Rate limiting on authentication endpoints to prevent brute-force attacks
  • Automated daily backups of user data

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — payment processing. See Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Render — cloud hosting. See Render's privacy policy.
  • Google (Gmail SMTP) — transactional email delivery.
  • County records portals — your configured portal (e.g. 1st NM Title, hALFILE). We act as an automated browser on your behalf using credentials you provide.

6. Cookies

We use a single HTTP-only session cookie (deed_token) to keep you logged in. This cookie does not track you across other websites. We do not use advertising or analytics cookies.

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Access your data — view your account info and search history in the app
  • Delete your account — contact us at joreveusa@gmail.com and we will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 7 days
  • Export your data — contact us to request a copy of your stored data
  • Update your information — update your email or password from within the app

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is removed within 7 days. Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics may be retained indefinitely.

Stripe may retain payment records for longer periods as required by financial regulations.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for professional use by adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify registered users via email. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at: joreveusa@gmail.com
