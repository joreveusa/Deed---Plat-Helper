PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

A certain tract of land near Llano Quemado, Taos County, New Mexico; within the Gijosa
Grant; located within projected Section 16, Township 24 North, Range 12 East, NMPM,
described as part of Lot 3, Block 32 of a private survey for the Ranchos Orchard and
Land Company; also described as part of Tract 47, Map 30, Survey 1, of the 1941 Taos
County Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes and bounds as
follows;

N 50°17'52" E, 275.02 feet. to the North corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;
