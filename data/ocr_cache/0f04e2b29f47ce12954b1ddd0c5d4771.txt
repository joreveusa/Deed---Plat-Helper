TRACT “C”

Martinez Grant; located within projected Sections 21 and 28, Township 26 North, Range
12 East, NMPM; described as part of Tract 8, Map 25, Survey 3, of the 1941 Taos
County Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes and bounds as
follows;

thence; S 56°16'00" W, 433.58 feet. to the SE corner of this tract, a 1/2 in. rebar set for the
true POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING, thence;

S 56°16'00" W, 192.95 feet. to a 1/2 in. rebar set on the northerly right-of-way of US

Highway 64, thence along said right-of-way;

found as a witness corner, bears S 42°58'13" E, 0.73 feet. distant, thence leaving said

right-of-way;
