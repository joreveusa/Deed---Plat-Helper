Tract "2"
A certain tract of land in Colonias, Taos County, New Mexico; within the Antonio
Martinez Grant; located within projected Section 35, Township 26 North, Range 12 :
East, NMPM; described as part of Parcel 4 shown on a survey plat entitled "Chacon,
Martinez, Garcia and Vargas", by George Rivera, NMLS no. 3149, dated 12/15/79;
also described as part of Tract 16, Map 84, Survey 2 of the 1941 Taos County
Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes and bounds as
follows;

triangulation station “Velvet”, a 1973 State Engineer Office brass cap monument
found bears S 62°44'19" E, 4309.25 feet. distant, thence;
S 47°12'59" W, 223.28 feet. to a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence; :
S 46°57'05" W, 139.33 feet. to the SW corner, a 1/2 In. rebar set, thence;
N 42°46'41" W, 259.67 feet. to the NW corner, a point at the center of a
50.00 feet. radius cul-de-sac, from whenceva 1/2 in. rebar set as witness corner

S 42°53'59" E, 259.80 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
