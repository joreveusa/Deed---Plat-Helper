Tract “B”

Serna Grant; located within projected Section 32, Township 25 North, Range 13
Fast, NMPM; described as part of Tract 16, Map 6, Survey 1 of the 1941 Taos County
Reassessment Survey and more particularly described by metes and bounds as

distant, thence leaving said road;
