Tract "2"
A certain tract of land in El Prado, Taos County, New Mexico; within the :
Taos Pueblo Grant; located within Section 32, Township 26 North, Range 13 East,
and within Section 5, Township 25 North, Range 13 East, NMPM; described as part of
Exception 130, Private Claim 23, Parcel 1 of the 1915 USGLO survey of the Taos Pueblo
Grant; also described as part of Tract 31, Map 6, Survey 2 of the 1941 Taos County
Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes and bounds as follows;
BEGINNING at the tie, at a 1915 USGLO brass cap monument found for Corner 4, Private
Claim 22, Parcel 1, thence; S 49°40'20" E, 87.63 feet. to the SW corner of this
tract, a 1/2 in. rebar set for the true POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING, thence;
N 22°49'28" E, 297.56 feet. to the NW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set on the westerly side
of "Eloy Gonzales Road", thence along said road;
S 42°15'37" E, 99.02 feet. to the NE corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence
leaving said road;
S 22°07'38" W, 287.93 feet. to the SE corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;
N 54°51'19" W, 33.57 feet. to a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;
N 24°34'05” W, 13.08 feet. to a point from whence a 1/2 in. rebar found as a witness
corner bears S 24°34'05" E, 2.00 feet. distant, thence;
N 49°40'20" W, 53.35 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
