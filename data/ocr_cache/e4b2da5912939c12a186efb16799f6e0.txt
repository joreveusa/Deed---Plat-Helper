PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

TRACT A-1
A certain tract of land near Arroyo Hondo, Taos County, New Mexico; within the
Antoine Leroux Grant; located within projected Section 5, Township 26 North, Range
12 East, NMPM; described as part of Tract 32, Map 49, Survey 4, of the 1941 Taos
County Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes and bounds
as follows;
BEGINNING at the SE corner of this tract, a 1/2 in. rebar set, from whence the
14-1/2 Mile Marker, a USGLO scribed stone monument found on the north boundary
of the Antonio Martinez or Godoi Grant bears S 59°11'04" E, 2421.54 feet. distant,
thence;

N 54° 17°00" W, 351.97 feet. to the SW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence;

N 10°45'49" W, 355.71 feet. to the NW comer, a 1/2 in. rebar set on the southerly

right-of-way of a county road, thence along said right-of-way;

S 54°17'00" E, 537.22 feet. to the NE corner, a 1/2 in rebar found, thence leaving

said right-of-way;

S 19°11'17" W, 255.50 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
This tract contains 2.500 acres, more or less; all as shown on a survey plat
entitled "Paul Hesch", RGSS survey no. L561-C-1A, by Scott B. Crowl, NMLS no.
12441, dated 06/22/04.
