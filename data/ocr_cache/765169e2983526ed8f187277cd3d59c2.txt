PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
Tract “A”
A certain tract of land near Llano Quemado, Taos County, New Mexico; within the
Cristoval de la Serna Grant; located within projected Section 2, Township 24 North,
Range 12 East, NMPM; described as part of Tract 29, Map 48, Survey 2, of the 1941
Taos County Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes and
bounds as follows;
BEGINNING at NW corner of this tract, a point from whence a 1/2 in. rebar set as a
witness corner, bears N 35°25'19" E, 20.00 feet. distant, also from whence
triangulation station “Quemado”, a 1973 State Engineer's Office brass cap monument
found bears N 09°45'32" W, 3357.95 feet. distant, thence;
S 85°02'00" E, 174.08 feet. to the NE corner, a point from whence a 1/2 in. rebar
set as a witness corner, bears N 35°25'29" E, 20.00 feet. distant, thence;
S 35°25'29" W, 366.17 feet. to the SE corner, a point on the northerly right-of-way
of State Road 68, from whence a 1/2 In. rebar found as a witness corner bears
S 35°25'29" W, 0,39 feet. distant, thence along said right-cf-way;
S 65°25'05” W, 297.40 feet. to the SW comer, a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence
leaving said right-of-way;
N 32°53'02" E, 30,66 feet. to a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;
N 35°25'19" E, 504.87 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
This tract contains 1.556 acres, more or less; all as shown on a survey plat
entitied "Weimer Properties, LLC", RGSS survey no. L1823-3B, by Scott B. Crowl,
NMLS no. 12441, dated 12/30/2004.
