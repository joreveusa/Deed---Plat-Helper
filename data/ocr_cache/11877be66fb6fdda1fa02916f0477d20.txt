PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
TRACT “1-A”
A certain tract of land near Liano Quemado, Taos County, New Mexico; within the
Gijosa Grant; located within projected Section 26, Township 25 North, Range 12
East, NMPM; described as part of Lot 12, Block 7 shown on a survey for the Ranchos
Orchard & Land Co., and also described as part of Tract 8, Map 46, Survey 2, of the
1941 Taos County Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes
and bounds as follows;
BEGINNING at a point at the centerline of a roadway and utility easement, 40 feet. in
width, from whence a 1/2 in. rebar found as a witness corner bears S 80°05'02" W,
20.10 feet distant, and from whence triangulation station “Cadillac,” a 1973 State
Engineer's Office brass cap monument found bears S 59°49'09" E, 3321.2 feet. distant,
thence leaving said centerline;
S 80°05'02" W, 168.62 feet. to a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;
S 89°55'23" W, 195.57 feet. to a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;
S 00°05'48" E, 197.90 feet. to the SE corner, a point at the centerline of an access
and underground utility easement 40 feet. in width, from whence a 1/2 in. rebar
found as a witness corner bears N 00°05'48" W, 20.05 feet. distant, thence;
S 89°54'55" W, 194.14 feet. to the SW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence;
N 00°06'40" W, 413.28 feet. to the NW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;
N 89°57'45" E, 483.92 feet. to a 1/2 in, rebar found, thence;

centerline;
S 14°06'30" E, 191.94 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
