PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
Tract “B-1b” ,
A certain tract of land near Ranchos de Taos, Taos County, New Mexico; within the
Gijosa Grant; located within projected Section 26, Township 25 North, Range 12
East, NMPM; described as part of Lot 12, Block 7 of a private survey for the Ranchos
Orchard & Land Co.; described as part of Tract 8, Map 46, Survey 2 of the 1941 Taos
County Reassessment Survey, and more particularly described by metes and bounds
as follows;
BEGINNING at the SE corner of this tract, a 1/2 in. rebar found on the southerly side
of Cactus Flower Road, from whence triangulation station ‘Cadillac,” a 1973 State
Engineer Office brass cap monument found, bears S 77°42'05" E, 2490.40 feet.
distant, thence along said road;
S 89°51'44" W, 165.18 feet. to the SW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence
leaving said road; ‘
N 00°10'23" W, 329.58 feet. to the NW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence;
N 89°53'03" E, 165.34 feet. to the NE corner, a point from whence a 1/2 in.
rebar set as a witness corner bears N 89°53'03" W, 1.00 feet. distant, thence;
S 00°08'45" E, 329.52 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
