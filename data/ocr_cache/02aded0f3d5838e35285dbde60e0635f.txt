TRACT “C2”

Martinez Grant; located within projected Section 2, Township 25 North, Range 12

County Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes and bounds

found bears S 80°35'38" W, 335.10 feet. distant, thence;

N 46°58'34".E, 234.60 feet. to the NW corner, a point at the centerline of
