PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

TRACT “B-1a”
A certain tract of land in Talpa, Taos County, New Mexico; partly within the Cristoval de la
Serna Grant; partly within USGLO Tract 55; located within projected Section 7, Township
24 North, Range 13 East, NMPM; described as part of Tract 83, Map 14, Survey 1, of the
1941 Taos County Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes and
bounds as follows;
BEGINNING for the tie at Corner 5, Tract 55, a USGLO brass cap monument found, thence
along the west boundary of the Rancho del Rio Grande Grant, S$ 36° 12' 26" E, 91.60 feet. toa
1/2 in. rebar found, thence leaving said boundary, N 30°52'34" E, 967.27 feet. to the SW
comer of this tract, a 1/2 in. rebar set for the true POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING,
thence;

N 30°52'34" E, 202.55 feet. to the NW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence;

S 59°07'26" E, 215.06 feet. to the NE comer, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence;

S 30°52'34" W, 202.55 feet. to the SE corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence;

N 59°07'26" W, 215.06 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.

Torres to Michael Salazar", RGSS survey no. L4316-2, by Scott B. Crowl, NMLS no. 12441,
dated 03/09/2004.
