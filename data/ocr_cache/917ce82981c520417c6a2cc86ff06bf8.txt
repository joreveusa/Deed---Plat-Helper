PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

Tract “B” (rev.)
A certain tract of land in Taos, Taos County, New Mexico; within the Cristoval de la
Serna Grant; located within projected Section 30,Township 25 North, Range 13 East,
NMPM; described as part of Tract 1, Map 31, Survey 2, of the 1941 Taos County
Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes and bounds as
follows;
BEGINNING at the NW corner of this tract, a 1/2 in. rebar set from whence
triangulation station “Bosque,” a 1973 State Engineer's Office brass cap monument
found, bears; S 87°41'57" E, 6734.5 feet. distant, thence;

S 36°33'45" E, 341.55 feet. to the NE corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found on the westerly

right-of-way of Gusdorf Road, thence along said right-of-way;

S 29°08'43" W, 219.34 feet. to the SE corner, a point from whence a 1/2 in. rebar

found as a witness corner bears N 29°08'43" E, 20.00 feet. distant, thence leaving

said right-of-way and along the northerly right-of-way of Roy Road;

An arc distance of 84.31 feet. along a 231.30 feet. radius curve to the right through

a 20°53'01" central angle, the chord of which bears N 50°18'31" W, 83.84 feet. to

a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;

N 39°51'52" W, 245.61 feet. to the SW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence;

N 29°14'29" E, 256.53 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
This tract contains 1.750 acres, more or less; all as shown on a survey plat entitled
"Cantu to Ott", RGSS survey no. L4485-1, by Scott B. Crowl, NMLS no. 12441, dated
12/07/2004.
