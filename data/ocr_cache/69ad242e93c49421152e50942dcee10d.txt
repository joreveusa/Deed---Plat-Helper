PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
Tract "B"
A certain tract of land in San Cristobal, Taos County, New Mexico; located within the
South 1/2 of Section 4 and the North 1/2 of Section 9, Township 27 North, Range 12
East, NMPM; described as part of Small Holding Claim 6035, Tract 1 of the 1913
USGLO survey of the Smail Holding Claims in Sections 4 and 9, T27N, R12E, NMPM;
also described as part of Tract 16, Map 7, Survey 5, of the 1941 Taos County
Reassessment Survey, and more particularly described by metes and bounds as
follows;
BEGINNING at the SW corner of this tract, a 1/2 in. rebar found, from whence
AP 6, a 1966 USBLM brass cap monument found on the westerly boundary of SHC
6035, Tr. 1 bears S 75°47'09" W, 637.29 feet. distant, thence;
N 12°37'51" W, 197.13 feet. to the NW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence;
N 83°54'14" E, 226.43 feet. to the NE corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set on the westerly ;
right-of-way of State Highway 522, thence along said right-of-way;
S 12°39'07" E, 190.44 feet. to the SE corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence leaving
said right-of-way;
S 82°12'57" W, 225.83 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
This tract contains 1.001 acre, more or less; all as shown on a survey plat entitled
"John Sheffield and Naomi Valdez", RGSS survey no. L4684, by Scott B. Crowl, NMLS
no. 12441, dated 07/20/2004.
