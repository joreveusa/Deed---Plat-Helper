PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

Tract “A” (rev.)
A certain tract of land in Taos, Taos County, New Mexico; within the Cristoval de la
Serna Grant; located within projected Section 30,Township 25 North, Range 13 East,
NMPM; described as part of Tract 1, Map 31, Survey 2, of the 1941 Taos County
Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes and bounds as
follows;
BEGINNING at the NE corner of this tract, a 1/2 in. rebar set from whence :
triangulation station “Bosque,” a 1973 State Engineer's Office brass cap monument
found, bears; S 87°41'57" E, 6734.5 feet. distant, thence;

S 29°14'29" W, 256.53 feet. to the SE corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set on the northerly

right-of-way of Roy Road, thence along said right-of-way;

N 39°51'52" W, 125.11 feet. to a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;

An arc distance of 113.66 feet. along a 315.96 feet. radius curve to the left through a

20° 36’ 36” central angle, the chord of which bears N 50° 15’ 54” W, 113.04 feet. to

the SW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence leaving said right-of-way;

N 29°14'29" E, 293.78 feet. to the NW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;

S 36°33'45" E, 250.00 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
This tract contains 1.402 acres, more or less; all as shown on a Survey plat entitled
"Cantu to Ott", RGSS survey no. L4485-1, by Scott B. Crowl, NMLS no. 12441, dated
12/07/2004.
