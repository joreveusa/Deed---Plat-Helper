PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
TRACT “N”
CUNNYNGHAM INDUSTRIAL PARK
A certain tract of land in Taos, Taos County, New Mexico; within the Cristoval de fa
Serna Grant; located within projected Section 19, Township 25 North, Range 13
East, NMPM; described as part of Tract 2, Map 31, Survey 2, of the 1941 Taos
County Reassessment Survey; and more particularly described by metes and bounds
as follows;
BEGINNING at the SE corner of this tract, a 1/2 in. rebar found on the westerly
right-of-way of Salazar Road Extension, from whence triangulation station “Bosque”,
a 1973 State Engineer’s Office brass cap monument found bears N 76°16'25" W,
9244.53 feet. distant, thence leaving said right-of-way;
N 39°16'25" W, 179.93 feet. to the SW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;
N 42°48'25” E, 230.27 feet. to the NW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found on the southerly
right-of-way of Bendix Drive, thence along said right-of-way;
S 47°29'53”" E, 178.92 feet. to the NE corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found at the intersection
of said right-of-way and the westerly right-of-way of Salazar Road Extension, thence
along said right-of-way;
An arc distance of 72.39 feet. along a 1950.00 feet. radius curve to the right through a
02°07'37" central angle, the chord of which bears S 42°12'02" W, 72.38 feet. to a 1/2
in, rebar found, thence; ;
S 43°15'50" W, 183.64 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
This tract contains 1.000 acres, more or less; all as shown on a survey plat
entitled "Whitney to West & Blaufuss"”, RGSS survey no. L4468-N, by Scott B. Crowl,
NMLS no. 12441, dated 06/24/04.
