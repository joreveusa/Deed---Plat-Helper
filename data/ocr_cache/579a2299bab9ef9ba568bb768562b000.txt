PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
Lot “4”, Tract “B”
A certain tract of land in the Los Cordovas area, near Ranchos de Taos, Taos County,
New Mexico; within the Gijosa Grant; located within projected Section 33, Township
25 North, Range 12 East, NMPM; described as part of Tract 9, Map 50, Survey 2, of
the 1941 Taos County Reassessment Survey; also described as part of Lot 1, Block
10 of a private survey for the Ranchos Orchard and Land Co., and more particularly
described by metes and bounds as foliows;

triangulation station “Godoi”, a 1973 State Engineer's Office brass cap monument
found, bears N 34°25'42” E, 627.31 feet. distant, thence;
N 88°55'04" E, 329.89 feet. to the NE corner, a 3/4 in. rebar found, thence;
S 00°39'42" E, 264.25 feet. to the SE corner, a point from whence a 1/2 in. rebar
found as a witness corner bears S 88°54'12" W, 11.00 feet. distant, thence;
S 88°54'12" W, 329.76 feet. to the SW corner, a point from whence a 1/2 in. rebar
found as a witness corner bears N 88°54'12" E, 11.00 feet. distant, thence;
N 00°41'21" W, 264.33 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
