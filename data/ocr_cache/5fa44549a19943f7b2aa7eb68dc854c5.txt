PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

Tract "7A"
A certain tract of land in the Colonias area, Taos County, New Mexico; within the Antonio
Martinez Grant; located within projected Sections 19 and 20, Township 26 North, Range 12
East, NMPM; described as part of Tract 6, Map 27, Survey 3 of the 1941 Taos County
Reassessement Survey and more particularly described by metes and bounds as follows;
BEGINNING at the SE corner of this tract, a 1/2 in. rebar set whence triangulation station
“Dunn”, a 1973 State Engineer's Office brass cap monument found bears N 14°55'21" E,
18349.78 feet. distant, thence;

N 76°43'37" W, 2521.85 feet. to the SW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence;

N 43°00'42" W, 384.43 feet. to the NW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence;

N 47°00'00" E, 1404.12 feet. to the NE corner, a 1/2 in. rebar set, thence;

S 42°53'52" E, 298.21 feet. to a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;

S 42°54'50" E, 2183.62 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.

"Patricio Martinez, Sr. Heirs", RGSS survey no. L4677, by Scott B. Crowl, NMLS no. 12441,
dated 06/03/2004.
