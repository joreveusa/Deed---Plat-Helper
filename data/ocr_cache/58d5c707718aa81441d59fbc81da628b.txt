TRACT “B2”
A certain tract of land in Colonias, Taos County, New Mexico; within the Antonio
Martinez Grant; located within projected Section 2, Township 25 North, Range 12
East, and projected Section 35, Township 26 North, Range 12 East, NMPM; described
as part of Tract 7, Map 24, Survey 2, of the 1941 Taos County Reassessment
Survey; and more particularly described by metes and bounds as follows;
BEGINNING at the SE corner of this tract, a 1/2 in. rebar set, from whence
triangulation station “Velvet”, a 1973 State Engineers Office brass cap monument
found bears S 12°57'49" W, 336.65 feet. distant, thence;
N 46°58'34”" E, 225.93 feet. to the NW corner, a point at the centerline of an
ingress, egress and utility easement, 30.00 feet in width, from whence a 1/2 in.
rebar found as a witness corner bears S 46°58'34" W, 15.00 feet. distant, thence
along said centerline;
S 44°21'06" E, 153.63 feet. to the NE corner, a point from whence a 1/2 in. rebar
set as a witness corner bears S 46°58'34" W, 15.00 feet. distant, thence leaving
said centerline;
S 46°58'34" W, 229.49 feet. to the SE corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence;
N 43°01'26" W, 153.59 feet. to the POINT AND PLACE OF BEGINNING.
