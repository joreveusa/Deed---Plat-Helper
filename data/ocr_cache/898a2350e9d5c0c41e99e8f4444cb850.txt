A certain tract of land in San Cristobal, Taos County, New Mexico; located within the
South 1/2 of Section 4, and the North 1/2 of Section 9, Township 27 North, Range
12 East, NMPM; described as part of Smail Holding Claim 6035, Tract 1 of the 1913
USGLO survey of the Small Holding Claims in Sections 4 and 9, T27N, Ri2E, NMPM;
also described as part of Tract 16, Map 7, Survey 5, of the 1941 Taos County
Reassessment Survey, and more particularly described by metes and bounds as
follows; :

N 40°18'45" W, 160.40 to the NW corner, a 1/2 in. rebar found, thence leaving
said SHC boundary;
